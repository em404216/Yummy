About us

YummyFood for Food Industries and Agricultural Products was established in 2019 in Ismailia, Egypt, as part of Al-Jaees Group.

Since its inception, the company has specialized in exporting frozen agricultural products, particularly IQF frozen strawberries, to markets in Brazil, Europe, and the Middle East.
